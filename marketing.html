Leading the social media channels and communications for Northeastern University's Global Experience Office from February 2023 to August 2023 provided me with some of my most impactful marketing experiences. Alongside managing a talented team of student workers, I also directed the Summer 2023 media fellowship program. Explore some of our noteworthy projects below:

Instagram Examples (with @northeasterngeo)

Some of our most successful Instagram campaigns were developed using Canva and incredible content provided by our talented students. I also occasionally used my own photography, like the time I posted about the charming local coffee shop I visited during my work trip in Mexico.

From the ever-popular DOC Instagram Takeover series to the captivating glimpses into the lives of fellows and student workers, each of the 60+ posts I worked on represents a visually compelling narrative about global experiences at Northeastern University.

TikTok Examples (with @northeasterngeo)

To begin expanding our recently established TikTok account, I recruited multiple student workers and initiated the content creation process by tapping into popular trends and CapCut templates. Our content strategy prioritized a blend of humor, practical tips, cultural insights, and engaging narratives tailored to resonate with our student audience.

Utilizing TikTok's dynamic features, we provided an immersive glimpse into the vibrant and enriching experiences awaiting participants in our programs. The results spoke volumes as we witnessed a remarkable surge in the seven months I worked led the account, with total account likes increasing by over 1100% and followers by more than 200%.

Newsletter Examples

Upon learning about the comprehensive rebranding initiative in mass communications, I guided our Events & Marketing Committee in reshaping our biweekly newsletter. The outcome, aptly named "The Postcard," proved to be a more potent communication tool than its predecessor. Utilizing Canva, I crafted a distinctive GIF that not only served as our logo but also enriched the newsletter, solidifying our new brand identity.

This revamped newsletter not only effectively conveyed essential information but also skillfully spotlighted key programming, delivering a visually appealing and cohesive narrative of the office's ongoing work to our 20,000+ mailing list.
