From social media takeovers to newsletter redesigns, I revitalized Northeastern University's Global Experience Office's communications channels, building a cohesive brand identity that resonated with 20,000+ students and driving dramatic growth across all platforms.

As Chair of the Events and Marketing Committee for the Global Experience Office from February to August 2023, I directed both strategy and execution across multiple platforms while managing a creative team of student workers and fellows. Here's how we made an impact:

Instagram (with @northeasterngeo)


Using Canva and incredible student-generated content, I led development for 60+ posts that balanced authentic storytelling with eye-catching design. From the beloved DOC Instagram Takeover series to behind-the-scenes glimpses of our fellows and student workers, each post contributed to a visually compelling narrative about global experiences at Northeastern.



TikTok (with @northeasterngeo)


Our TikTok account was in its early stages when I began leading content strategy in early 2023. I recruited and trained student workers to utilize trending formats and CapCut templates, developing content that blended humor, practical study abroad tips, and cultural insights. Our most successful post came from a video I created during a work trip to Mexico, demonstrating the power of authentic, in-the-moment storytelling.

The Results: In seven months, total account likes increased by 1100% and followers by 200%.



Newsletters


When Northeastern announced a university-wide rebranding initiative, I led the transformation of our biweekly newsletter into "The Postcard," working with our Events & Marketing Committee to reshape our communication approach.

Using Canva, I designed a distinctive GIF that served as both our logo and a visual anchor throughout each edition, solidifying our new brand identity. This revamped newsletter effectively conveyed essential information while strategically spotlighting key programming, delivering a visually cohesive narrative of our work to 20,000+ subscribers.
